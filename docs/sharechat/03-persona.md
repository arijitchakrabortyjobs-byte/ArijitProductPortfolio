# Personas: Who We're Building For

## Why These Personas
I didn't invent personas from imagination. These three emerged directly from the research — they represent the three distinct creator segments that experience the earnings visibility problem differently, and therefore need different things from the same dashboard.

## Persona 1: Priya — The Aspiring Regional Creator
- **Who:** 24, Indore, creates Hindi comedy skits and motivational content, 45K followers
- **Device:** Mid-range Android on 4G (this matters for load time and app size decisions)
- **Core frustration:** She has no idea how much she earned last week. She lost ₹12,000 to a scam agency that promised monetization help. She sees Instagram creators flaunting their earnings dashboards and wonders why ShareChat doesn't have one.
- **What she needs from us:** A simple, mobile-first earnings view that answers "how much did I make this month?" in under 3 seconds. Per-video earnings so she knows what content to double down on. Agency safety ratings so she doesn't get scammed again.
- **Why she matters:** Priya represents 820,000 nano/micro creators. If we can't retain this segment, the content pipeline dries up.

## Persona 2: Ravi — The Established Creator
- **Who:** 31, Hyderabad, Telugu lifestyle and food content, 300K followers, cross-posts to Instagram
- **Core frustration:** He can't generate a professional performance report for brand sponsors. He has no audience demographics. He suspects his audience skewed younger after a viral video but can't confirm it. He's considering going Instagram-only because the tools are better.
- **What he needs from us:** Exportable analytics reports, audience demographic breakdowns, and cross-platform comparison so he can make an informed decision about where to invest his time.
- **Why he matters:** Ravi represents the 80,000+ mid-to-mega creators who are the platform's content moat. Losing even 10% of this segment to Instagram would be catastrophic.

## Persona 3: Meera — The Agency/Talent Manager
- **Who:** 28, Mumbai, manages 15 regional creators across ShareChat and Moj
- **Core frustration:** She manually collects screenshots from each creator. No aggregated view. Can't prove campaign ROI to brand clients with credible data.
- **What she needs from us:** A portfolio dashboard showing all her creators' performance, with exportable reports for clients.
- **Why she matters:** Agencies are a multiplier. If we give Meera good tools, she brings 15 creators (and their brands) with her. But she's a V2 persona — Priya and Ravi come first.

## Prioritization Decision
I'm building for Priya first (Sprint 1-4), Ravi second (Sprint 5-7), and Meera as a V2 follow-up. The reasoning is simple: Priya's problem is the most urgent (she's the one getting scammed and churning), and her needs are a subset of Ravi's. Everything we build for Priya also works for Ravi — we just layer on professional tools later.
